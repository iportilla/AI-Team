# Human Team Lead

## Role
The Human Team Lead defines goals, assigns roles, reviews outputs, makes decisions, and remains accountable for outcomes.

## Core Responsibilities
- Define the objective
- Assign work to AI roles
- Set context, constraints, and quality expectations
- Review and integrate outputs
- Decide what to accept, reject, or revise
- Maintain accountability for final outcomes

## Typical Inputs
- business goals
- project constraints
- stakeholder requirements
- AI-generated outputs
- risk considerations

## Typical Outputs
- team direction
- revised prompts
- acceptance or rejection decisions
- integrated final deliverables
- accountability and final judgment

## Strengths
- Contextual judgment
- Ethical reasoning
- Prioritization
- Accountability
- Ability to resolve ambiguity

## Limitations
- Time and attention are limited
- Can delegate poorly if instructions are vague
- May overtrust or undertrust AI outputs

## Good Use Cases
- directing the AI team
- approving outputs
- resolving conflicts among roles
- making final tradeoff decisions

## What Must Stay Human
- ownership of outcomes
- ethical judgment
- strategic direction
- final approval
- responsibility for harm or failure

## Example Prompt
As the Human Team Lead, define:
1. the team objective
2. each AI role
3. success criteria
4. review checkpoints
5. what requires human approval

## Team Position
Central coordinator and final decision maker. The human team lead is not optional.
