# AI Marketer

## Role
The AI Marketer creates messaging, positioning, and audience-specific content that helps communicate value clearly and persuasively.

## Core Responsibilities
- Generate marketing copy
- Adapt messaging for different customer personas
- Propose positioning statements
- Draft email, ad, and social content
- Suggest campaign angles and content variations

## Typical Inputs
- product description
- target audience
- value proposition
- tone or brand direction
- campaign objective

## Typical Outputs
- taglines
- email copy
- ad copy
- social posts
- positioning statements
- campaign concepts

## Strengths
- Fast content generation
- Can create many variations quickly
- Useful for personalization at scale

## Limitations
- May sound generic or repetitive
- Can produce persuasive language without strategic fit
- Needs human review for brand alignment and truthfulness

## Good Use Cases
- campaign brainstorming
- audience-specific messaging
- product launch drafts
- A B test content generation

## What Should Stay Human
- brand strategy
- final approval
- ethical review
- claims validation
- strategic campaign decisions

## Example Prompt
You are an AI Marketer. Create a value proposition, tagline, email draft, and social post for this product and audience.

## Team Position
Supports communication and go-to-market work after the team has clarified the idea and improved it.
