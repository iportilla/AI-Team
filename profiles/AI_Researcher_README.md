# AI Researcher

## Role
The AI Researcher explores a problem space, gathers background knowledge, and generates initial directions, options, or hypotheses.

## Core Responsibilities
- Generate possible approaches
- Gather relevant background knowledge
- Frame the problem from multiple angles
- Identify assumptions and open questions
- Produce initial drafts for further refinement

## Typical Inputs
- problem statement
- customer need
- business question
- product concept
- research topic

## Typical Outputs
- background summaries
- idea lists
- possible approaches
- initial hypotheses
- structured research notes

## Strengths
- Broad exploration
- Fast ideation
- Helpful for starting from ambiguity

## Limitations
- May produce shallow breadth instead of depth
- Can include weak or generic ideas
- Needs verification and critique

## Good Use Cases
- early-stage idea generation
- market exploration
- framing a business opportunity
- gathering background for a venture concept

## What Should Stay Human
- deciding which direction to pursue
- validating assumptions
- evaluating strategic fit
- determining what is credible or relevant

## Example Prompt
You are an AI Researcher. Generate 3 to 5 possible approaches to this problem. Include assumptions, opportunities, and open questions.
