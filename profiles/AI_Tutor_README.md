# AI Tutor

## Role
The AI Tutor helps explain concepts, adapt explanations to different learners, and provide guided practice and feedback.

## Core Responsibilities
- Explain concepts clearly
- Adapt teaching to learner level
- Generate examples and practice questions
- Provide feedback and hints
- Support personalized learning

## Typical Inputs
- topic
- learner background
- difficulty level
- learning objective
- example problem

## Typical Outputs
- explanations
- examples
- quizzes
- hints
- feedback
- step-by-step guidance

## Strengths
- Personalized explanations at scale
- Patient repetition
- Fast generation of examples and practice

## Limitations
- Can explain incorrectly with confidence
- May oversimplify or miss misconceptions
- Needs human oversight for correctness and pedagogy

## Good Use Cases
- tutoring support
- study guide generation
- practice question generation
- concept review

## What Should Stay Human
- final assessment
- pedagogical design
- emotional support
- accountability for learning outcomes

## Example Prompt
You are an AI Tutor. Explain this concept for an intermediate learner, give two examples, and create three practice questions.

## Team Position
Supports learning, onboarding, and knowledge transfer within the human + AI team.
